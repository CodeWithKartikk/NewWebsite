Repairable

Repairable parts can be repaired rather than being completely replaced. They are designed to be modular and require affordable maintenance. This enables the spare parts to be easily removed, repaired and replaced at a cheaper cost.


Consumable

Spare parts that cannot be repaired are called consumable. They are available at a lower cost and in large volumes.
